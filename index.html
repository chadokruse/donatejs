Our Mission:

A donate button next to every mention of a nonprofit

We envision a world where any citizen of the Internet can donate to any nonprofit mentioned anywhere on the Internet. She should be able to do so confidently and securely, in one click, as close to zero cost as possible, regardless of her payment method and where her charitable assets are held.
